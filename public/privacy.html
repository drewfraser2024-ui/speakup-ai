Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to SpeakUp AI ("we," "our," or "the app"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

By using SpeakUp AI, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the app.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the minimum information needed to provide and improve the speech training experience.

a) Information You Provide

  • Onboarding preferences — your learning goal, topics of interest, preferred feedback tone, and purpose (e.g., interviews, networking)
  • Speech content — text transcriptions of your spoken responses during practice sessions
  • Game activity — vocabulary match answers and scores

b) Information Collected Automatically

  • Anonymous device ID — a randomly generated identifier stored on your device (not linked to your real identity)
  • Session data — mode used, difficulty level, duration, scores, and performance metrics
  • Voice metrics — pitch, volume, pace, pause count, and filler word detection (processed locally on your device)
  • Vocabulary history — accuracy, times, and missed words across games
  • Modal preferences — whether you chose to skip instructional pop-ups

c) Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, photos, or any other personally identifiable information. We do not store audio recordings — all voice processing happens in real time on your device using your browser's built-in Speech Recognition API.

3. How We Use Your Information

DataPurpose
Onboarding preferencesPersonalize prompts, AI feedback tone, and difficulty
Speech transcriptionsSend to AI for real-time feedback and scoring (not stored permanently)

Voice metricsProvide tone analysis and speaking improvement suggestions

4. Data Storage & Security

Local Storage (Primary)

All your data is stored locally on your device using your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device unless cloud sync is active.

Cloud Sync (Supabase)

If cloud sync is available, your anonymized preferences, session history, and vocabulary progress are synced to a secure Supabase (PostgreSQL) database. Data is linked only to your anonymous device ID — never to personal information.

We use industry-standard security measures to protect data in transit and at rest. However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Third-Party Services

SpeakUp AI uses the following third-party services:

  • Groq API — processes your speech transcriptions to generate AI feedback and scores. Transcriptions are sent to Groq's servers for processing and are subject to Groq's Privacy Policy.
  • Supabase — provides cloud database storage for anonymous session data. Subject to Supabase's Privacy Policy.
  • Vercel — hosts the application. Subject to Vercel's Privacy Policy.
  • Web Speech API — your browser's built-in speech recognition. Audio is processed by your browser provider (e.g., Google for Chrome). We do not control this processing.

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any other third parties.

6. Microphone Access

SpeakUp AI requests access to your device's microphone for speech recognition during practice sessions.

  • Microphone access is only active during practice sessions and only when you initiate recording
  • Audio is processed in real time by your browser's Speech Recognition API and is not recorded or stored by our app
  • You can revoke microphone access at any time through your browser settings

7. Cookies & Tracking

SpeakUp AI does not use cookies, tracking pixels, analytics scripts, or advertising trackers. We use only browser localStorage to save your preferences and progress locally on your device.

8. Children's Privacy

SpeakUp AI is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with data, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

9. Your Rights & Data Control

You have full control over your data:

  • View your data — all your data is stored in your browser's localStorage and can be inspected via browser developer tools
  • Delete your data — clear your browser's localStorage to remove all app data from your device
  • Reset preferences — clearing localStorage will reset all onboarding choices, progress, and modal preferences
  • Revoke microphone access — manage permissions in your browser settings at any time

If you need cloud-synced data deleted, please contact us using the information below.

10. Data Retention

Local data persists until you clear your browser storage. Cloud-synced data is retained to provide continuity across sessions. You may request deletion of cloud data at any time by contacting us.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of SpeakUp AI after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
